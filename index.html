Frequently Asked Questions

Clarity of your journey

Who is eligible to apply?

High-potential African leaders aged 18-28 from humble backgrounds who are intellectually curious and adaptable.

What is the duration of the fellowship?

It is a 6-month journey including virtual workshops, in-person modules and mountain-based experiences.

Is the mountain climb mandatory?

While mountain climbing is a part of our heritage and the literal 'Climb4Dreams' experience, we focus on both literal and symbolic climbs. Participation in all core modules is required.

What is the cost of the fellowship?

The fellowship is fully funded for selected participants. This includes all training, mountain expedition costs, accommodation, and meals during in-person modules. Travel to Kenya may be subsidized based on demonstrated need.

When are applications due?

Applications for the 2026 cohort are now open. We review applications on a rolling basis, so early submission is encouraged. Selected candidates will be notified within 4-6 weeks of submission.

What does the fellowship include?

The fellowship includes: 6-month leadership curriculum, Mt. Kilimanjaro expedition, Mawazo Hub innovation sessions, mentorship from experienced leaders, community service projects, cultural immersion experiences, and lifetime alumni network access.
